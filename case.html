WASTE WINNERS

UNs 12th goal for sustainable development is too limit food waste globally. The concept behind Waste Winners is to bring us one step closer to achieve said goal. This visualization takes inspiration from gamification trends to encourage individuals to save food before it is spoiled.

INSTANT GRATIFICATION & GAMIFICATION –

By letting the user see their direct impact allows the user to feel proud, rewarded and encourages a good behaviour. Repeating this behaviour eventually evolves into the user repeing benefits such as coupons.

LOGOTYPE –

The rate of the food’s expiration is color coded. By saving food close to its expiration, the user earns more points which in turn leads to greater rewards.
